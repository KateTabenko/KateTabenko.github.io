DIRECTIONS

1. In a medium microwave-safe bowl, melt the peanut butter and chocolate and stir until it is well mixed. Add the powdered sugar to the chocolate and peanut butter mixture.

2. In a separate mixing bowl, pour 4 cups of the cornflakes and stir in the peanut butter & chocolate mixture and mix until the cornflakes are evenly coated.

3. Add the remaining cereal 1 cup at a time until you reach your desired consistency. (For this batch, 7 cups were used but some require more, some require less).

4. Scoop the cornflake mixture into lined muffin tins and shape into a nest using your fingers (careful! It may be hot!) or the back of a spoon.

5. Place 4 mini eggs into each nest.

6. Refrigerate the peanut butter nests for at least 30 minutes before serving or digging in so that the chocolate has time to set properly.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 6-8 cups cornflakes
  • 6oz chocolate (we used Hersheyâ€™s kisses)
  • 64 Mini eggs (or small candy of your choice)
